# Initial Planning

Use this workflow when `/plan-step <step-number>` resolves to a live step folder with `requirement.md` present and no `step.md` yet.
- Create or refresh `step.md` for the resolved step only.
- Keep the new `step.md` aligned with the same-folder `requirement.md`.
- Do not edit any other step folder.
- Do not decompose a discovered requirement into additional step folders during planning.

See @.opencode/workflows/baicai-vc/plan-step/common.md
